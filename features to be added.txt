For this system, I’d focus on features that reduce manual follow-up, improve trust in payment records, and make each role feel like the product is built for them.

For admins:
- Approval workflow rules: require dual review for large payments, flagged receipts, or suspicious duplicates.
- Role-based analytics: see collections by campus, class, term, staff member, and payment channel.
- Broadcasts and campaign messaging: send targeted reminders by balance range, class, or overdue status.
- Audit dashboards: highlight unusual actions like mass deactivations, repeated password resets, or many rejected receipts.
- Fee planning tools: simulate how a new fee structure affects balances before publishing it.

For accounts staff:
- Smart verification queue: sort by highest amount, oldest pending, duplicate risk, OCR mismatch, or missing reference.
- Side-by-side receipt review: show extracted OCR fields next to submitted fields with mismatch highlights.
- Bulk actions with safeguards: approve low-risk clean receipts in batches, but require notes for risky ones.
- Reconciliation tools: mark payments as matched to bank statements or mobile money exports.
- Exception handling inbox: one place for flagged receipts, missing proof, duplicate references, and partial payments.

For students:
- Real payment timeline: submitted, verified, approved, rejected, with who reviewed and any notes.
- Balance forecast: show how much remains this term and suggested installment milestones.
- Downloadable fee statement / clearance letter: useful for registration and exams.
- Payment reminders with action links: overdue notices that take them straight to upload payment.
- Receipt quality helper: warn before upload if the image is blurry, cropped, dark, or missing key fields.

For all roles:
- In-app and email notifications.
- Search everywhere: students, references, receipts, users, reports.
- Better profile/account settings: password change, profile photo, session history.
- Export improvements: CSV, PDF, print-ready summaries.
- Mobile-first receipt upload and review polish.

If I were prioritizing the next 5 highest-value additions, I’d pick:
1. Smart verification queue for accounts
2. Student payment timeline with review notes
3. Statement/clearance PDF generation
4. Reconciliation against bank/mobile-money exports
5. Targeted reminder campaigns for admins
